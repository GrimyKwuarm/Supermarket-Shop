# Contributing

## Editing Content
- Recipe cards and shopping items live in `app.js`.
- Keep item names supermarket-friendly.
- Put items into the most likely PAK'nSAVE section.

## Testing
- Open `index.html`.
- Try desktop and mobile widths.
- Tick and untick shopping items.
- Change servings and confirm quantities update.
- Use reset and confirm the checklist clears.
